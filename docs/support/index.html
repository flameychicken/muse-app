Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a TrackCard with Muse?

Open the Muse app and tap “Scan TrackCard.” Hold your iPhone near the NFC card until you feel a vibration or see the playlist appear. Make sure NFC is enabled in your phone settings.

My TrackCard isn’t scanning. What should I do?

Ensure you are using a compatible iPhone (iPhone 7 or newer), hold the card near the top/back of your phone, and avoid metal interference. If the problem persists, contact us at privacy@muse-app.info.

How can I favorite or delete a playlist?

Once a playlist is scanned and added to your library, long-press it to reveal the options menu where you can favorite or delete it.

How do I connect my Spotify account?

Go to the “Settings” tab and press “Connect to Spotify.” A QR code or login screen will guide you through the Spotify authentication process.

Can I write to my own NFC cards?

Currently, Muse only supports reading from pre-encoded TrackCards. Writing functionality is planned for future updates.

Where can I get TrackCards?

TrackCards are sold through our official store and authorized resellers. Stay tuned to our website or app announcements for availability.

Still need help?

If your issue isn’t listed above, you can reach us directly at privacy@muse-app.info. We usually respond within 1–2 business days.
