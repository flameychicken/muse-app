Welcome to Muse

Muse lets you share your favorite playlists with a single tap—no typing, no scanning codes. It writes your Spotify playlist URL onto an NFC‐enabled card (or keychain), so anyone can tap and listen instantly.

Download from the App Store now:

Muse works like this:

  • Create a “MuseTag” by choosing one of your Spotify playlists.
  • Muse writes that playlist onto any blank NFC card (or keychain).
  • Share your card. Others simply tap it to open your playlist instantly in their Spotify app.
