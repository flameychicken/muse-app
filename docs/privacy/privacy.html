Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Muse. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you provide. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data (or, in this case, do not handle data) when you use our app or visit our website.

Information We Don’t Collect

Muse does not collect, store, or share any personal information. You can use our app features—including connecting your Spotify or Apple Music accounts, scanning or creating MuseTags—without providing any personal data directly to us.

  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • No precise location data or device identifiers.
  • No browsing history or analytics beyond basic server logs.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Spotify and Apple Music to allow you to browse and manage your playlists. When you connect your Spotify or Apple Music account, you authenticate directly with the respective service. Muse does not store your Spotify or Apple Music credentials or your personal data from these services. Any calls to their APIs are made using your own access tokens, and you can revoke access at any time via your Spotify or Apple Music account settings.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook pixel, or other tracking scripts on our website. Basic server logs (e.g., IP address, timestamp, requested URL) may be stored by our hosting provider for debugging and maintenance, but are not tied back to you personally.

Cookies & Local Storage

We do not utilize cookies or local storage to track you personally. If you see any cookie prompts, they are likely from our hosting provider or static site generator—they are not used for user tracking, personalization, or advertising.

Children’s Privacy

Our app and website are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal data to us, please contact us, and we will promptly delete it.

Security

Although we do not store any personal user data, we employ standard security measures (HTTPS/TLS) to protect any communication between your device and our servers. Always ensure you are running the latest version of the Muse app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. The “Effective Date” at the top will reflect when changes are made. If we make material changes, we will post a notice on our homepage (muse-app.info) or email you if you have opted in to communications.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please email us at privacy@muse-app.info.
